# Deleting a space

To delete a space, you need the `{{ roles-speechsense-admin }}` role for the space.

To delete a space:

1. Open the {{ speechsense-name }} [home page]({{ link-speechsense-main }}).
1. Go to the space of your choice.
1. In the top-right corner, click **{{ ui-key.yc-ui-talkanalytics.common.more }}** and select ![delete](../../../_assets/console-icons/trash-bin.svg)Â **{{ ui-key.yc-ui-talkanalytics.common.delete-key-value }}**.
1. Confirm the space deletion.

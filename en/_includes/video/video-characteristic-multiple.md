You can upload videos in various supported formats, such as [MP4](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MP4_file_format), [AVI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Audio_Video_Interleave), [MKV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matroska), [FLV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flash_Video), [MOV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/QuickTime_File_Format), [WebM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WebM), etc. You can [download](../../video/operations/video/upload.md) videos either one by one or several at a time.
